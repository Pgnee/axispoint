For Patients

A Steady Point of Reference for Your Care

What to Expect

We know imaging appointments can feel unfamiliar. Here's what sets your visit with aXisPoint apart.

Subspecialist Reads
Every exam is read by a fellowship-trained subspecialist — the right expert for your specific study.
Minimally Invasive Options
Through our affiliation with VISA, many conditions can be treated with outpatient, image-guided procedures — often avoiding traditional surgery.
Close to Home
Based at Grandview Medical Center, so your imaging and follow-up care stay close and coordinated.

Where to Find Us

Grandview Medical Center
aXisPoint Radiology is located on the Grandview Medical Center campus. Parking is available on-site; please allow extra time to park and check in.

Preparing for Your Visit

Before You Arrive
  • Bring a photo ID and your insurance card
  • Bring your physician's imaging order, if provided
  • Arrive early to allow time for check-in
  • Follow any prep instructions provided when scheduling
Insurance & Records
We work with a range of insurance plans. Please contact us ahead of your visit with any questions about coverage, billing, or transferring prior imaging records so your care team has what they need.

Ten Fellowship-Trained Subspecialists

Every exam at aXisPoint Radiology is read by a physician trained specifically in that area of imaging.

Our practice is built on subspecialty matching: rather than a single radiologist reading every type of exam, studies are directed to the physician whose fellowship training aligns with the clinical question at hand. This means a musculoskeletal MRI is read by a musculoskeletal-trained radiologist, a body CT by a body-imaging-trained radiologist, and an interventional procedure by an interventional radiologist — giving referring physicians and patients confidence in every report.

Our Team

Our Care Team

Behind every physician is a dedicated care team ensuring exams run smoothly and patients feel supported at every step, from scheduling through final report. Our care team includes Brenea Black, who helps coordinate patient and referring physician needs across the practice.

As aXisPoint grows, our care team will continue to expand alongside our physician roster to keep pace with Birmingham's imaging needs.

Subspecialty Matching

Every exam ordered through aXisPoint Radiology is routed to the fellowship-trained subspecialist best suited to interpret it — whether that's a diagnostic radiologist with musculoskeletal, neuroradiology, or body imaging training, or an interventional radiologist for image-guided procedures. This matching process is central to how our practice delivers accurate, clinically relevant reports for every referring physician and patient.

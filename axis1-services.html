Full-Spectrum Imaging Services

From general radiography to advanced interventional procedures, aXisPoint Radiology offers the complete range of imaging modalities under one subspecialized practice.

Our physicians are fellowship-trained subspecialists who interpret every study within their area of focused expertise. Below are the imaging modalities available through aXisPoint Radiology at Grandview Medical Center.

CT

Computed Tomography

Cross-sectional imaging that produces detailed views of internal organs, bone, soft tissue, and blood vessels. CT is often used for rapid evaluation in both routine and emergency settings.

MRI

Magnetic Resonance Imaging

Uses magnetic fields and radio waves to generate highly detailed images of soft tissue, the brain, spine, joints, and organs without ionizing radiation.

X-ray

General Radiography

The fastest and most widely used imaging tool, providing quick evaluation of bones, chest, and other structures for a broad range of clinical needs.

Ultrasound

Diagnostic Sonography

Real-time, radiation-free imaging using sound waves, commonly used to evaluate abdominal organs, vascular flow, and soft tissue structures.

Mammography

Breast Imaging

Specialized low-dose X-ray imaging used for breast cancer screening and diagnostic evaluation, interpreted by fellowship-trained breast imaging subspecialists.

Nuclear Medicine

Molecular Imaging

Uses small amounts of radioactive tracers to evaluate organ function and metabolic activity, supporting diagnosis across cardiology, oncology, and beyond.

Interventional Radiology

Image-Guided Procedures

Minimally invasive, image-guided treatments performed by our interventional radiologists, offering an alternative to traditional surgery for many conditions.

Affiliated Care

Vascular & Interventional Care

aXisPoint Radiology is affiliated with Vascular & Interventional Specialists of America (VISA), an outpatient vascular and interventional lab offering a full range of minimally invasive vascular & interventional treatments. Through this affiliation, patients have access to advanced, image-guided care for vascular conditions close to home, including uterine fibroids and peripheral artery disease (PAD).

Uterine Fibroid Embolization (UFE)
Prostate Artery Embolization (PAE)
Genicular Artery Embolization (GAE)
Varicose Vein Treatment
Vertebral Augmentation

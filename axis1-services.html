Full-Spectrum Imaging & Vascular Care

Our Modalities

Seven imaging modalities, all read by fellowship-trained subspecialists at Grandview Medical Center.

CT
Computed Tomography
Fast, detailed cross-sectional imaging used for a wide range of diagnostic questions, from trauma to cancer staging.
MRI
Magnetic Resonance Imaging
High-resolution imaging of soft tissue, joints, and the brain without ionizing radiation.
X-ray
General Radiography
Quick, widely available imaging for bones, chest, and general diagnostic screening.
Ultrasound
Diagnostic Sonography
Real-time, radiation-free imaging used for abdominal, vascular, and obstetric evaluation.
Mammography
Breast Imaging
Screening and diagnostic breast imaging read by subspecialty-trained radiologists.
Nuclear Medicine
Molecular Imaging
Imaging that visualizes function at the molecular level to support diagnosis and staging.
Interventional Radiology
Image-Guided Procedures
Minimally invasive, image-guided treatments performed as an alternative to traditional surgery.

Vascular & Interventional Care

Our physicians also treat vascular disease directly through Vascular & Interventional Specialists of America (VISA), aXisPoint's affiliated outpatient lab. VISA offers minimally invasive, image-guided treatment — often avoiding traditional surgery and getting patients home the same day.

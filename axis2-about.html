About aXisPoint Radiology

A subspecialized diagnostic and interventional radiology group at Grandview Medical Center — built around one idea: precision has a point.

Who We Are

aXisPoint Radiology is the diagnostic and interventional radiology group based at Grandview Medical Center, 3570 Grandview Parkway, Suite 100, Birmingham, AL 35243. Our ten fellowship-trained subspecialists and dedicated care team read every study — and perform every procedure — with the depth of expertise a subspecialty demands, not the breadth of a generalist practice.

We founded aXisPoint on a simple mission: Precision Has a Point. Every image, every report, every intervention is an opportunity to be exact — for the referring physician who needs a confident answer, and for the patient who needs the right one.

The Subspecialized Model

Generalist radiology groups spread a handful of readers across every modality and body system. We do the opposite.

01

Subspecialty Matching

Every study is routed to the fellowship-trained subspecialist best suited to read it — neuroradiology to neuroradiologists, body imaging to body imagers, vascular cases to interventionalists.

02

Direct Physician Contact

Referring physicians can reach the radiologist who read the case directly, not a call center. Questions get answered by the person who has the images in front of them.

03

Full-Spectrum Coverage

From CT and MRI to nuclear medicine and interventional radiology, our subspecialists cover the complete range of imaging and image-guided treatment under one roof.

Our Affiliations

aXisPoint Radiology practices in affiliation with two organizations that extend our reach — one in vascular and interventional treatment, one in national radiology infrastructure.

Vascular & Interventional Specialists of America

Our interventional radiologists provide minimally invasive vascular & interventional treatments through VISA, our affiliated outpatient vascular lab — including uterine fibroid embolization (UFE) and treatment for peripheral artery disease (PAD).

Coaxion Radiology

Our physicians practice within Coaxion's national network of 180+ radiologists, giving aXisPoint the resources, technology, and scale of a national platform with the responsiveness of a local practice.

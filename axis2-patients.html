For Patients

Here for You, Every Step

What to Expect

We know imaging can feel unfamiliar. Here's how a visit with us usually goes.

When you arrive at Grandview Medical Center for your exam, our care team will check you in, confirm your study, and walk you through what to expect before you're brought back for imaging.

Your images are read by a fellowship-trained subspecialist matched to your specific exam, and results are sent to your ordering physician, who will discuss them with you.

Preparing for Your Visit

Before You Arrive

Follow any preparation instructions provided when your exam was scheduled — some studies require fasting or adjusted medications.

What to Bring

A photo ID, your insurance information, and a list of current medications help your visit go smoothly.

What to Wear

Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing is recommended. You may be asked to change depending on the exam.

Insurance & Records

Our team can help coordinate insurance questions and prior imaging records — just ask when you check in.

Questions Before Your Visit?

Our care team is happy to help you prepare.

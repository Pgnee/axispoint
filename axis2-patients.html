Vascular & Interventional Care with VISA

aXisPoint's physicians treat vascular disease directly through Vascular & Interventional Specialists of America (VISA), our affiliated outpatient vascular and interventional radiology lab. Through VISA, patients have access to minimally invasive vascular & interventional treatments that use imaging guidance instead of traditional surgery, often as same-day, outpatient procedures. Whether it's uterine fibroids, an enlarged prostate, knee pain, vein disease, or a spine fracture, VISA's specialists focus on getting patients back to their lives faster.

Preparing for Your Visit

Before You Arrive

Follow any preparation instructions provided when your exam was scheduled — some studies require fasting, hydration, or adjusted medications. Bring a photo ID and your insurance card, and arrive a few minutes early to check in.

Insurance & Records

Our team can help coordinate insurance questions and locate prior imaging records from other facilities — just let us know when you check in or call ahead of your visit.

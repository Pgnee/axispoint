What We Offer

Full-Spectrum Imaging Services

Seven modalities, one subspecialized team. Every exam is read by a fellowship-trained radiologist matched to that specific study type.

Computed Tomography

CT

Fast, detailed cross-sectional imaging used to evaluate injuries, internal organs, and a wide range of conditions.

Magnetic Resonance Imaging

MRI

High-resolution imaging of soft tissue, joints, and the brain and spine, without ionizing radiation.

General Radiography

X-ray

The fastest, most accessible imaging exam — used for bones, chest, and initial evaluation of many conditions.

Diagnostic Sonography

Ultrasound

Real-time, radiation-free imaging using sound waves — often used for abdominal, vascular, and prenatal evaluation.

Breast Imaging

Mammography

Screening and diagnostic breast imaging read by subspecialty-trained breast radiologists.

Molecular Imaging

Nuclear Medicine

Imaging that captures how tissues and organs function, supporting evaluation across a range of conditions.

Image-Guided Procedures

Interventional Radiology

Minimally invasive, image-guided treatments performed by our physicians — an alternative to traditional surgery for many vascular and other conditions.

Vascular & Interventional Care

Our physicians also treat vascular disease directly through Vascular & Interventional Specialists of America (VISA), aXisPoint's affiliated outpatient vascular/IR lab — minimally invasive, image-guided, outpatient care.

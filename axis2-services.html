Imaging & Treatment

Full-Spectrum Imaging Services

From routine X-rays to advanced interventional procedures, aXisPoint Radiology offers the complete range of imaging and image-guided treatment — every study read by a fellowship-trained subspecialist.

Our Modalities

Seven imaging and treatment modalities, available at Grandview Medical Center, each staffed by physicians trained specifically in that discipline.

CT

Computed Tomography

Fast, detailed cross-sectional imaging used to evaluate trauma, cancer staging, chest and abdominal disease, and vascular anatomy.

MRI

Magnetic Resonance Imaging

High-resolution imaging of the brain, spine, joints, and soft tissue without ionizing radiation, read by subspecialty-trained radiologists.

X-ray

Radiography

The fastest imaging option for bone, chest, and general diagnostic evaluation — often available with same-day results.

Ultrasound

Sonography

Real-time imaging for abdominal, vascular, obstetric, and soft-tissue evaluation with no radiation exposure.

Mammography

Breast Imaging

Screening and diagnostic breast imaging performed and interpreted by fellowship-trained breast imaging specialists.

Nuclear Medicine

Molecular Imaging

Functional imaging that visualizes how organs and tissues are working, supporting cancer, cardiac, and thyroid evaluation.

Interventional Radiology

Image-Guided Treatment

Minimally invasive, image-guided procedures performed by our interventional radiologists — an alternative to open surgery for many vascular and oncologic conditions.

Vascular & Interventional Care

Our interventional radiologists also treat vascular disease directly, in affiliation with Vascular & Interventional Specialists of America (VISA), aXisPoint's affiliated outpatient vascular lab.

Treated Through VISA

Patients referred through aXisPoint have access to a full range of minimally invasive vascular & interventional treatments performed by our physicians at VISA, including options for uterine fibroids and peripheral vascular disease.

Uterine Fibroid Embolization (UFE) Prostate Artery Embolization (PAE) Genicular Artery Embolization (GAE) for Knee Osteoarthritis Varicose Vein Treatment Vertebral Augmentation for Spine Fractures

Breana Black is a board-certified Adult-Gerontology Acute Care Nurse Practitioner who supports the vascular and interventional care team, helping coordinate treatment and recovery for patients undergoing image-guided procedures. Her background in critical care nursing gives her a steady, detail-oriented approach to patient assessment, peri-procedural care, and family communication.

Education

  • B.S. in Nursing, Jacksonville State University (2018)
  • M.S. in Nursing, Adult-Gerontology Acute Care Nurse Practitioner track, Jacksonville State University (2024)

Certifications

  • Board Certified Adult-Gerontology Acute Care Nurse Practitioner (AGACNP-BC), American Nurses Credentialing Center
  • Critical Care Registered Nurse (CCRN)
  • ACLS and BLS Certified

Professional Experience

  • Adult-Gerontology Acute Care Nurse Practitioner, vascular & interventional care team (2024–present)
  • Critical Care Registered Nurse, Grandview Medical Center Cardiovascular ICU (2018–2024)
  • ECMO Specialist, University of Alabama at Birmingham (2021–2022)
  • Clinical Adjunct Instructor, Jacksonville State University (2020–2021)

Clinical Focus

Breana's clinical background is rooted in acute and critical care, and she brings that experience to the vascular and interventional side of practice — supporting patients before and after procedures, guiding recovery, and making sure patients and their families understand each step of care.

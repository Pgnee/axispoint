Services

Full-Spectrum Imaging Services

From routine radiography to advanced image-guided procedures, aXisPoint Radiology offers the full range of diagnostic and interventional imaging at Grandview Medical Center — every study interpreted by a fellowship-trained subspecialist.

CT
Computed Tomography

High-resolution cross-sectional imaging for rapid, detailed evaluation of the body — from trauma and cancer staging to routine diagnostic workups.

MRI
Magnetic Resonance Imaging

Detailed, radiation-free imaging of soft tissue, joints, the brain and spine — read by the subspecialist whose training matches the anatomy in question.

X-ray
General Radiography

Fast, accessible imaging for fractures, chest complaints, and everyday diagnostic needs, with same-day turnaround on routine studies.

Ultrasound
Diagnostic Sonography

Real-time, radiation-free imaging for abdominal, vascular, and soft-tissue evaluation, including guidance for minimally invasive procedures.

Mammography
Breast Imaging

Screening and diagnostic breast imaging interpreted by our fellowship-trained breast imaging subspecialist, from annual screening to biopsy guidance.

Nuclear Medicine
Molecular Imaging

Functional imaging that visualizes how organs and tissues are working, supporting diagnosis and treatment planning across a range of conditions.

Interventional Radiology
Image-Guided Procedures

Minimally invasive, image-guided treatments performed by our fellowship-trained IR physicians in partnership with VISA — an alternative to open surgery for many conditions.

Vascular & Interventional Care

Our interventional radiologists partner with VISA (Vascular & Interventional Specialists of America) to deliver minimally invasive, image-guided treatments for a wide range of vascular and structural conditions — often as an outpatient alternative to traditional surgery.

Uterine Fibroid Embolization (UFE) Prostate Artery Embolization (PAE) Genicular Artery Embolization (GAE) Varicose Vein Treatment Vertebral Augmentation
